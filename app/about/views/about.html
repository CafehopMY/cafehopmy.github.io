About CafeHop KL

Back in September 2013, we produced over 5000 physical maps with 28 cafes and 4 sponsors and without them, we would not be here today. (See here) (Link to the archived physical map section here).

Our objective is to connect cafe/coffee lovers, cafes and coffee together around Malaysia. We do this with our digital map, an annual project, a soon-to-come app and everything else in between.
