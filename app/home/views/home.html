CafeHop KL is a project mapping independent cafes around Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. We have compiled a map of the best cafes in the Klang Valley for your cafe hopping adventures. The map is on this site in its digital form, and starting 21st Sept 2013 , we will have printed copies distributed among the 28 cafes that have supported this project. Check them out!
